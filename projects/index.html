Global Archive of Election Administration

I founded the Global Archive of Election Administration (GAEA) in 2024 to centralize dates and data for all elections in human history. Visit global-aea.org to track our progress as we prepare the database for a full public launch.

Florida Archival Election Project

The State of Florida maintained no historical record of state election results by precinct until 2012 and has never collected local election results. Since 2024, I have traveled to election offices across the state to document and archive all available election results, including by precinct and for local office, as far back as possible. As of July 2025, I have successfully archived results for 15 of 54 counties. The goal is to create a free public archive of digitized Florida election results.
