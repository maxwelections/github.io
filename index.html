Welcome to My Website

I am a first-year PhD student studying American and Comparative Politics at the University of Florida. I specialize in local election administration and voter turnout, both in the United States and abroad. My recently published paper was the first statewide study of Florida's municipal elections, accounting for 92% of all localities.

I have been an active member of UF's Election Data Science Lab since 2021, and I created the Lab's logo. Visit the Lab website by clicking on the ballot box below.
